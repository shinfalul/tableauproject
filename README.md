Project Summary

This project analyzes an Online Food Delivery dataset to understand overall business performance, restaurant trends, customer preferences, and delivery partner efficiency.
The dashboards provide insights on revenue patterns, top-performing restaurants, best-selling items, and delivery performance to support data-driven decision-making.

Tableau Public Link

https://codebasics.io/challenges/codebasics-resume-project-challenge/23

Key Insights

Revenue peaks in April and dips during monsoon months.

North Indian, South Indian, and Pizza are the most preferred food categories.

A few restaurants and delivery partners contribute to the majority of revenue and order volume.
